Tactics
Use walls
Crushing Weight TOP hits for 5 when the target is against a wall. You don't need to set this up — corners and corridors do the work. Look for enemies already pinned by the dungeon layout.
Break things
Destroying obstacles unlocks bonuses: Firebombs BOT deals 2 damage to all enemies adjacent to the destroyed obstacle, The Big One BOT blesses you, Knockout the Support BOT strengthens you. If there's something to break, breaking it is usually the right call.
Elements need a round
You generate [fire] or [earth] at the end of your turn — you can't spend it yourself until the following round. Play a generating card early so the element is ready for a consuming card next round.
Robotic Enhancement
Your most reliable control card. Attack 2 + {stun} on any enemy, no conditions required. Use it on the enemy that's causing the most trouble.
Save your burns
Explosive Blitz BOT and The Big One TOP are both [BURN] — gone for the scenario once used. Firebombs is fully reusable. Hold the burns for the moments where the payoff is biggest.
Combos
Generate → Spend Fire
Play Explosive Blitz TOP, Firebombs TOP, or The Big One TOP to create [fire]. Next round, spend it on Piston Punch TOP for attack 3, push 3.
Generate → Spend Earth
Knockout the Support TOP or The Big One TOP creates [earth]. Next round, spend it on Implode TOP (attack 4 + {muddle}) or Piston Punch BOT (stun + {poison}).
Watch Out For
Crushing Weight BOT
The bottom action requires the target to be adjacent to both you and a wall. If the wall condition isn't met, the effect does nothing — check the layout before committing.
